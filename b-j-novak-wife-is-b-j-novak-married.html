Who Is B.J Novak?

Benjamin Joseph Novak is an actor and TV writer, known for appearing as Ryan Howard in the NBC sitcom The Office.

Born on July 31, 1979, in Massachusetts, Novak graduated from Harvard University, where he studied English and Spanish literature.

His journey into entertainment began with performing stand up comedy in clubs.

Novak later went on to write and starr in The Office  between 2005 and 2013.

Since then, he has appeared in many films such as Inglourious Basterds (2009), Saving Mr. Banks (2013), and The Founder (2016), and Vengeance (2022).

Other film projects in which he contributed to include The Mindy Project, The Newsroom, The Premise, The Amazing Spider-Man 2, and several others.

In 2023, Novak was signed in by Disney to adapt Sleeping Beauty as “a live action script.”

Novak has received many awards and accolades including five Primetime Emmy Award nominations and two Screen Actors Guild Awards.
